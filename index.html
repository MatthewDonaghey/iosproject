Project Overview

TiltFlyer is a 2D arcade iOS game where players control a flying object through gaps in pillars using tilt controls. Featuring haptics, Game Center leaderboards, and colorful customizable visuals, it's a fast-paced and immersive mobile experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

What devices support TiltFlyer?

Any iOS device running iOS 15 or higher.

How do I unlock new flying objects?

Earn in-game currency by playing and use it to buy custom objects in the customization menu.

Does TiltFlyer require internet?

Internet is only needed for Game Center leaderboard access and help page viewing.

Can I turn off the haptic feedback?

Yes, you can adjust haptics and sound in the settings screen.

Is there a pause option during gameplay?

Yes! Just tap the screen with two fingers to pause the game and bring up the pause menu.
